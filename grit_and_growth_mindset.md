# Grit and Growth Mindset 


## 1. Grit

**Summary:**  

Grit means not giving up on your goals.  
Even if things are hard, you keep trying until you succeed.

---

## 2.  Growth Mindset

**Summary:**

Growth mindset means believing you can become better by learning and practicing.  
Mistakes help you grow.

---

## 3. Internal Locus of Control

**What is it?** 
 
It means you believe your results come from your own actions.

**Main Point:**  

Focus on what you can control — your effort and your attitude.  
Do not blame luck or other people.

---

## 4. How to Build Growth Mindset

- Believe you can improve.
- Try new challenges.
- Learn from mistakes.
- Say “I will learn” instead of “I can’t”.
- Accept feedback.
- Practice daily.
- Be patient.

---

## 5. My Actions as a Software Developer

- Code every day.
- Fix errors calmly.
- Ask doubts.
- Learn from others.
- Read documentation.
- Build small projects.
- Check my progress.
- Do not compare with others.
- Focus on learning.
- Stay consistent.
