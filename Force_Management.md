# Focus Management

## 1. Deep Work

- Deep Work = focused, distraction-free work.  
- Helps you learn fast and produce better results.  

## 2. Deep Work Duration

- Work in focused sessions (short enough to stay sharp).  

## 3. Deadlines

- Deadlines improve focus and productivity.  

## 4.1 How to Do Deep Work

- Schedule distraction-free time.  
- Focus on one task at a time.  
- Minimize social media.  
- Take breaks to recharge.  

## 4.2 How to implement these principles daily

- Set fixed deep work hours in a day.
- work in quiet place without distractions.
- Limit checking emails or social media during work hours.

## 5. Social Media Risks

- Wastes time and attention.  
- Reduces focus.  
- Increases stress and anxiety.  

## 6. Diffused Mode (Brain Rest)

- Meditation, walking, or sitting quietly.  
- Lying down or doing focused chores.  

## 7. Starting Difficult Tasks

1. Work for 30 seconds, then take a break.  
2. Remind yourself: "I can do this."  
3. Repeat and gradually increase time.  
4. Builds confidence. 

- This builds proof and confidence in your ability.  
- You can use this for scary or boring tasks.  
- Over time, it shifts your identity to a capable and focused person.
