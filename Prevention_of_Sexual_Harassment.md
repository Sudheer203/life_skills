# Prevention of Sexual Harassment

## 1. What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?

* Unwanted physical contact like touching, hugging, or patting.

* Verbal comments about someone’s body, appearance, or sexual jokes.
  
* Sending inappropriate messages, emails, or images.

* Making threats or demands with sexual intent.
  
* Showing offensive materials at work.

* Repeatedly asking someone out after they say no.

## 2. What would you do if you face or witness any incident or repeated incidents of such behaviour??

* Speak up if you feel safe and tell the person to stop.
  
* Keep records of the incidents (dates, times, messages).
  
* Report it to HR or the designated internal committee.
  
* Support the person if you witness harassment.
  
* Follow the company’s official procedure for complaints.
  
* Avoid confronting the harasser alone if it feels unsafe.
