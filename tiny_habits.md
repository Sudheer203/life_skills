# Tiny Habits & Atomic Habits

---

## 1. Tiny Habits – BJ Fogg

### Question 1: Most interesting idea

* The most interesting idea is to start very small.  
* Do tiny habits like 1 push-up or read 1 page.  
* Small habits are easy, so we don’t feel lazy to do them.

---

## 2. Tiny Habits – Core Message

### Question 2: What is B = MAP?

From the book Tiny Habits:

* B = Behavior  
* M = Motivation  
* A = Ability  
* P = Prompt  

Behavior happens when:
- You are motivated  
- You can do it easily  
- You have a reminder  


---

### Question 3: Why Celebrate (Shine)?

Celebration makes your brain feel happy.  
When you feel happy, your brain remembers the habit.  
That is how habits grow.

You can:
- Smile  
- Say “Yes!”  
- Feel proud  

---

## 3. 1% Better Every Day

### Question 4: Most interesting idea

Improve just 1% every day.  
Small daily progress becomes big success later.  
Consistency is more important than big effort.

---

## 4. Atomic Habits

### Question 5: Identity Idea

Focus on who you want to become.

Don’t say: “I want to exercise.”  
Say: “I am becoming a healthy person.”

Habits build identity.

---

### Question 6: How to Make a Habit Easy?

Make it:
- Obvious  
- Attractive  
- Easy  
- Satisfying  

Example:
- Keep book on table to read.  
- Start with 2 minutes only.  
- Prepare things before time.

---

### Question 7: How to Make a Habit Hard?

To stop a bad habit:
- Make it invisible  
- Make it unattractive  
- Make it hard  
- Make it unsatisfying  

Example:
- Keep phone away.  
- Turn off notifications.  
- Delete apps.

## 5. Reflection
