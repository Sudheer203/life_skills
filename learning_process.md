### Question 1: What is the Feynman Technique?

**Answer (1 line):**  
The Feynman Technique is a way to learn better by explaining a topic in simple words using your own understanding.

## 2. Learning How to Learn – TED Talk by Barbara Oakley

**Video:** [Learning How to Learn](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=O96fE1E-rf8) (17:50 min)

### Question 2: Most Interesting Story or Idea

**Answer:**  
The idea that our brain has two thinking modes was very interesting. One mode is for deep focus, and the other helps us think freely when we relax.
### Question 3: Active and Diffused Modes of Thinking

- **Active (Focused) Mode** – Deep concentration and step-by-step thinking.  
- **Diffused Mode** – Relaxed thinking that helps connect ideas.

## 3. Learn Anything in 20 Hours

**Video:** [Learn Anything in 20 Hours](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5MgBikgcWnY) (20 min)

### Question 4: Steps to Approach a New Topic

1. Choose the topic  
2. Break it into small parts  
3. Learn the basics
4. Avoid distractions  
5. Practice for at least 20 hours  
6. Commit to regular practice  

---

## 4. Learning Principles in the Bootcamp

### Key Principles

- Give **100% focus** while learning  
- Programming needs **deep attention**  
- Focus improves with practice  
- Practice **deep work**  
  - https://doist.com/blog/deep-work/  
- Study properly, relax properly   
- Keep phone on silent during work  
- Remove non-work notifications  
- Block social media during work hours  
- Track time using apps like **Boosted**

---

### Question 5: Actions to Improve Your Learning Process

Some actions I can take:

- Study concepts deeply, not in a hurry  
- Explain concepts in my own words  
- Practice coding regularly  
- Take notes and revise them  
- Ask questions when stuck  
- Reduce distractions during study time  
- Track my time and progress  
- Teach others to learn better
