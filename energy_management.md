# 4. Sleep is Your Superpower

## Key Points

-   Sleep improves memory
-   Sleep repairs the body
-   Sleep balances hormones
-   Sleep improves mood
-   Lack of sleep causes health problems
-   Sleep improves focus
-   Sleep boosts immunity
-   Sleep helps in weight control

## How to Sleep Better

-   Sleep at same time daily
-   Avoid phone before bed
-   Keep room dark
-   Avoid caffeine at night
-   Exercise regularly
-   Eat light dinner
-   Relax before sleep
